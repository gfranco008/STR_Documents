This Short-Term Rental Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into between Gustavo (“Host”) and the Guest (“Renter”) as of the reservation date confirmed through Airbnb or an authorized booking platform. By completing a reservation, submitting payment, and/or occupying the property, Guest agrees to all terms outlined below.

1. Property

The property (“Property”) is located in Augusta, Georgia and is provided exclusively for short-term residential lodging.

2. Term of Stay

Check-in: 3:00 PM

Check-out: Before 11:00 AM

Unauthorized late check-out may result in additional fees, including but not limited to extended cleaning charges or costs caused by impact to the next reservation.

3. Occupancy & Guests

Maximum occupancy is 4 guests. Only registered guests are permitted on the property unless prior written approval is provided by Host.

Unauthorized guests may result in immediate termination of the stay without refund.

4. Payments, Fees & Platform

All payments, fees, deposits, and applicable taxes are processed through the booking platform or other authorized reservation channel.

This property is professionally managed using Hospitable, which may be used for guest communication, automation, reservation support, and operational management.

5. Security, Damages & Additional Charges

Guest is fully responsible for any damage, loss, excessive cleaning, or excessive wear caused during the stay, including but not limited to:

  • Damage to furniture, appliances, walls, flooring, fixtures, linens, or décor
  • Missing or damaged household items
  • Excessive cleaning, including pet-related cleaning
  • Smoking remediation or odor removal
  • Costs caused by unauthorized late check-out or rule violations

All charges will be documented and submitted through the booking platform when applicable.

6. House Rules

Guest agrees to follow the rules below. Violations may result in immediate termination of stay without refund and additional charges.

  • No smoking inside the property
  • No parties, events, gatherings, or disruptive behavior
  • Maximum occupancy of 4 guests at all times
  • Dogs are allowed, but are not permitted on beds or couches
  • Guest must clean up after pets, both inside and outside
  • No unregistered guests without prior approval
  • Respect neighbors and maintain reasonable noise levels

7. Use of Property

The property is intended for residential lodging only. Commercial activity, event hosting, illegal activity, or unsafe conduct is strictly prohibited.

8. Property Condition & Care

Guest agrees to maintain the property in a clean and orderly condition, use appliances and furnishings responsibly, and promptly report any damage, malfunction, or safety concern to Host.

9. Surveillance & Security Disclosure

For the safety and security of guests and the property, exterior security cameras may be in use and may be positioned at entry points and/or outdoor areas.

No cameras are located inside the home or in private areas.

By booking, Guest acknowledges and consents to the presence of exterior monitoring devices.

10. Liability Waiver

Guest acknowledges that use of the property is at their own risk. Host is not responsible for personal injury, accidents, loss, theft, or damage to personal belongings, except where prohibited by applicable law.

11. Indemnification

Guest agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Host from claims, damages, losses, liabilities, costs, or expenses arising from Guest’s use of the property, violation of this Agreement, or actions of Guest’s invitees, pets, or unauthorized visitors.

12. Right of Entry

Host reserves the right to access the property for maintenance, inspection, safety, or emergency purposes, with reasonable notice when possible.

13. Termination

Host reserves the right to terminate this Agreement immediately if Guest violates any terms. In such cases, no refunds will be issued and additional charges may apply.

14. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and interpreted in accordance with the laws of the State of Georgia.

15. Agreement Acceptance

By booking and/or occupying the property, Guest confirms that they have read, understood, and agreed to all terms of this Agreement.
