# Short-Term Rental Agreement

**Belair Stay – Augusta, GA**

This Short-Term Rental Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into between **Gustavo (“Host”)** and the **Guest (“Renter”)** as of the reservation date confirmed through Airbnb or an authorized booking platform.

By completing a reservation, submitting payment, and/or occupying the property, Guest agrees to all terms outlined below.

---

## 1. Property

The property (“Property”) is located in Augusta, Georgia and is provided exclusively for short-term residential lodging.

---

## 2. Term of Stay

Check-in: 3:00 PM
Check-out: Before 11:00 AM

Unauthorized late check-out may result in additional fees, including but not limited to extended cleaning charges or the cost of the next reservation.

---

## 3. Occupancy & Guests

Maximum occupancy: **4 guests**

Only registered guests are permitted on the property unless prior written approval is provided by Host. Unauthorized guests may result in immediate termination of the stay without refund.

---

## 4. Payments, Fees & Platform

All payments, fees, and applicable taxes are processed through the booking platform.

This property is professionally managed using **Hospitable**, which may be used for guest communication, automation, and operational support.

---

## 5. Security, Damages & Additional Charges

Guest is fully responsible for any damage, loss, or excessive wear caused during the stay, including but not limited to:

* Damage to furniture, appliances, or fixtures
* Missing items
* Excessive cleaning (including pet-related cleaning)
* Smoking remediation

All charges will be documented and submitted through the booking platform.

---

## 6. House Rules (Strictly Enforced)

Guest agrees to the following:

* No smoking inside the property
* No parties, events, or gatherings
* Maximum occupancy of 4 guests at all times
* Dogs are allowed, but **not permitted on beds or couches**
* Guest must **clean up after pets (inside and outside)**
* No unregistered guests without prior approval
* Respect neighbors and maintain reasonable noise levels

Violation of these rules may result in immediate termination of the stay without refund and potential additional fees.

---

## 7. Use of Property

The property is intended for residential use only. Any commercial activity, event hosting, or unlawful conduct is strictly prohibited.

---

## 8. Property Condition & Care

Guest agrees to:

* Maintain the property in a clean and orderly condition
* Use appliances and furnishings responsibly
* Report any issues promptly

---

By booking, Guest acknowledges and consents to the presence of exterior monitoring devices.
